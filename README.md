# 🎓 Strengthening Haitian Children's Education for Sustainable Development

**Organization:** Open Hearts Training Center (OHTC) — Cap-Haitian, Haiti

> Empowering 500 vulnerable children in northern Haiti through career orientation, emotional development, and community engagement.

## 📋 Project Description

This socio-educational project serves **500 children aged 6–14** in the communes of **Acul-du-Nord** and **Limonade**, Haiti. Delivered through structured after-school workshops, the program introduces children to career pathways, teaches goal-setting, and builds essential life skills.

Children participating in this program will:
- Discover and explore diverse career opportunities
- Learn to express emotions and resolve conflicts peacefully
- Engage with environmental protection and sustainability
- Develop critical thinking and community problem-solving skills
- Build aspirations and long-term personal goals

## 🎯 Objectives

| # | Objective |
|---|-----------|
| 1 | Train **15 teachers** in career orientation methodology |
| 2 | Establish **5 community clubs** serving 500 children |
| 3 | Distribute **1,000 books** to participants |
| 4 | Achieve a **25% improvement** in career awareness among children |
| 5 | Introduce children to **diverse career pathways** |
| 6 | Engage students in projects addressing **real-life community challenges** |

## 👥 Target Audience

**Vulnerable children aged 6–14** in Acul-du-Nord and Limonade, Haiti.

## 🗺️ Geographic Coverage

- 📍 Acul-du-Nord, Nord Department, Haiti
- 📍 Limonade, Nord Department, Haiti
